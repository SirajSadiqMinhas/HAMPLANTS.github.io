About Us

Landscape Designers | Consultants | Plant Producers | Plant Rentals & Maintenance Services | Janitorial Services

Company Overview

Established in 2005 by Mr. Muhammad Amjad Minhas & Mr.Muhammad Akmal Minhas, H.A.M Services (previously known as HAM Plants care) was established and still operating from Rukhanwala, near Kasur. Since its inauguration many new concepts have been introduced i.e use of boulders.



Featuring a stock of over 35,000 plants of various species boasting a wide range of indigenous and adaptive flora, the HAM Plant Care farm is maintained on a daily basis by a team of over 12 workers under the guidance and supervision of Mr. Akmal Minhas.



We are the main plant maintenance contractor to many prestigious hotels, offices, factories and residential properties. All of our plants are put through an internal Quality Assurance (QA) test, prior delivery to our customers



Our design philosophy is to provide a healthy, comfortable, beautiful and sustainable environment. Our work is inspired by unique customer driven designs that are not only beautiful but also efficient. We give priority to the use of natural resources, owing to their ingrained depth and enduring qualities



Landscaping is both a science and an art, and requires good observation and design skills. H.A.M Services understands the elements of nature and construction and blends them accordingly.



We are a company totally dedicated to environmental ethos, and shall ensure the continuation of a safe, clean and healthy ecosystem. We also have solid business processes that are geared towards a greener environment.



Recently with addition of Mr. Ajmal Minhas (having 24 years of experience in Banking and Textile industry), one of the founders of the company, we have started the janitorial service for our major clients. We have well trained janitorial staff along with supervisors and the cleaning equipment is provided as and when required. We provide 1 supervisor for a company having 10 janitorial staff along with our visit to every client on weekly basis.
