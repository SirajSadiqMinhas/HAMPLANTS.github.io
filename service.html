H.A.M Plant care is an integral part of H.A.M Service. Along with landscaping, M/s H.A.M Service also started undertaking the interiorscaping of various corporate office spaces for whom it provides monthly maintenance services and also providing janitorial services. These services, may take the form of plant rental or monthly maintenance contracts and include:

  • Provision of skilled gardeners
  • Covers the cost of Equipment charges
  • Ensures periodic substitution of plants to keep them in a healthy state as and when required
  • Covers Transportation Charges for the movement of plants being replaced
  • And lastly, ensures supervision by a representative of M/s H.A.M Services
  • 1 supervisor is provided to any company who avails 10 or more janitorial staff
  • Weekly visit of the management team to every client
