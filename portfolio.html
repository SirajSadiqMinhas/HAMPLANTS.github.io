Landscaping Projects

Since establishment, HAM Plant Care has had the privilege of carrying out many gardening and landscaping projects on turn-key basis also under the expertise of Mr. Amjad Minhas. It’s sales point hosts full-fledged horticultural facilities along with a variety of trees, bushes, creepers, ground covers, etc. in all types, sizes and configurations. We have the capacity of developing from traditional residential gardens as small as 200 sqft. to 50 acres of an industrial parks; we’ve had the pleasure of making the world greener with our own personal unique touch that extends to corporate sect or and public squares. Some of our key projects are mentioned below:

Client M/S Diamond paint factory
Work Details Newly established required landscaping of over 3000 square yards. Earth filling, compaction & fumigation of compost, etc: 40500 cft. Establishment of grass lawns over 2500 sq yards. Contouring of main central lawn over 3 ft. variation in levels. Over 450 plants, trees and bushes were emplaced. Waterproofing of roof of an underground tank which was converted into a garden.
Maintenance Contract From 2017 till Dated this garden was maintained under the personal supervision of Mrs.Muhammad Amjad Minhas by the qualified staff of HAM Plant Care.
Client SAPPHIRE (PVT) LTD.
